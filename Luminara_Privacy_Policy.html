Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

We respect your privacy and are committed to full transparency about how your data is handled.

Local Data

The app stores most of your data locally on your device, including:

  • Saved and favorited quotes
  • Language and theme preferences
  • Reminder settings
  • Daily streak data
  • Content and mood preferences

Server-Stored Data

If you choose to sign in with an account (Google, Apple, or another provider), we store the following data on a secure server:

  • Your display name
  • Your email address
  • Account identifier (OAuth ID)
  • Sign-in method
  • Last sign-in timestamp

This data is used solely for account identification and is not shared with any third parties.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. We do not use any tracking, analytics, or advertising tools.

Data Deletion

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings.

Quote Attribution

Quotes in this app are attributed to their original authors. This app is not sponsored by or affiliated with any of the quoted authors.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at luminara.app@gmail.com.
