Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Luminara ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when using the Luminara mobile application.

1. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data and only when necessary.

Local Data (Stored on Your Device)

  • Saved and liked quotes
  • Language and theme preferences
  • Reminder settings
  • Daily streak and usage preferences
  • Content interests and personalization data

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Account Data (Optional)

If you choose to sign in using Apple, Google, or another provider, we may store:

  • Name (if provided)
  • Email address
  • Authentication identifier (OAuth ID)
  • Sign-in method
  • Last login timestamp

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used only to:

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Sync your account across devices (if logged in)
  • Personalize your experience

We do not use your data for advertising, tracking, or analytics.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party authentication providers (such as Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In). These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain account-related data only as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and associated data directly from within the app settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Luminara is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
